Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service in Port Melbourne?

We cover all of Port Melbourne and surrounding suburbs including Albert Park, South Melbourne, and Garden City. Our service radius extends to approximately 15km from the Port Melbourne CBD.

How long does a typical painting project take?

Project duration varies based on scope and complexity. A standard three-bedroom home interior typically takes 3-4 days, while exterior projects may take 4-7 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What types of paint do you use?

We exclusively use premium paints from trusted manufacturers like Dulux and Taubmans, specifically chosen for each project's requirements. All our paints are low-VOC and environmentally responsible.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide a 7-year warranty on all our painting work, covering both workmanship and materials. This warranty is fully transferable if you sell your property.

What preparation work is included?

Our comprehensive prep work includes surface cleaning, sanding, crack repair, hole filling, and proper priming. We also protect all surrounding surfaces and furniture before beginning any paint application.
